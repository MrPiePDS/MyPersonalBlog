Advanced Privacy & Security

At Snappy, we believe your conversations should be as private as face-to-face interactions. Our advanced security features ensure your messages stay between you and your intended recipients.

  • End-to-end encryption for all messages and calls
  • Two-factor authentication for account protection
  • Disappearing messages with customizable timers
  • Screen security to prevent screenshots
  • Biometric lock for additional app security

Rich Media Sharing

Share your moments and important files with ease. Snappy supports all types of media with high-quality preservation and fast delivery.

  • High-resolution photo sharing without compression
  • 4K video sharing with preview thumbnails
  • Document sharing for all file types up to 2GB
  • Voice messages with waveform visualization
  • Live location sharing with real-time updates

Seamless Group Collaboration

Snappy makes group communication efficient and organized, whether you're planning a family event or managing a work project.

  • Create groups with up to 1000 members
  • Advanced admin controls and member permissions
  • Pinned messages for important information
  • Polls and voting for quick decision making
  • Shared calendars and event planning
